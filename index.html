About Us

Casa Del Chelo is a premium staycation destination located in the scenic city of Tagaytay in the Philippines. Surrounded by breathtaking natural beauty, our staycation offers a serene and tranquil atmosphere that is perfect for relaxation and rejuvenation.


Our staycation is designed to provide our guests with a comfortable and enjoyable experience. With 3 well-sanitized rooms, all equipped with air conditioning, guests can enjoy a relaxing stay. We offer a range of cooking facilities, including a rice cooker, electric kettle, and induction cooker, as well as a water dispenser and refrigerator for added convenience. Guests can stay entertained with a TV with access to Netflix, Wi-Fi, and a Platinum Videoke set with Bluetooth. For relaxation, we have a swimming pool, kubo and garden set, and a barbeque grill. Guests are welcome to use our kitchen facilities and utensils to prepare their meals.
